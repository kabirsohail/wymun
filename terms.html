2. Organization Information

Youth Impacts Model United Nations (YIMUN) is an international educational organization dedicated to promoting diplomacy, global awareness, and leadership development through Model United Nations conferences. YIMUN operates conferences in major cities including New York City and Washington, D.C.

3. Eligibility and Registration

3.1 Age Requirements

  • Participants must be between 16-25 years of age at the time of conference registration
  • Delegates under 18 must provide parental consent and supervision arrangements
  • Faculty advisors must be at least 21 years of age

3.2 Registration Process

  • All participants must complete official registration through the YIMUN portal
  • Registration requires accurate personal information, academic credentials, and emergency contacts
  • Delegates must select their preferred country assignments and committee preferences
  • Registration fees are non-refundable after the stated deadline

4.1 Academic Integrity

4.2 Behavioral Standards
